Biography

Mohsen Chavoshi is an Iranian singer, composer, songwriter and music arranger. He was born on July 30, 1979 in the city of Khorramshahr. Chavoshi’s Family is originally from the Region of of Kurdistan/Iran. However, his parents moved to Khorramshahr before he was born. Forced by the devastation caused by Iran-Iraq war his family moved between various towns and cities including Kermanshah, Karaj, Mashhad and Tehran for the first two decades of his life. Chavoshi believes that specially his time in Mashhad’s refugee camp has had an enormous influence on him and has made him a stronger as a person.
Chavoshi has three brothers and two sisters. He got married in March 2009 and has a son named Zankow who was born 3 years after his marriage.

Personal life

Chavoshi’s interest in music surfaced when he was a teenager and his professional career began after he finished the military service. He dropped out of college to follow his dream of becoming a musician. His first album “Curse” was released in 2004. In the next two years he made three more albums named “Suicide is Forbidden”, “A Shoe Bale” and “I'm Sorry”.

Since the release of his first albums, Chavoshi gained public recognition and many producers presented willingness to collaborate with him. His first official album, “A Lotus Sprout”, was released in October of 2008 by “Avaye Barbad” with a license from Ministry of Culture and Islamic Guidance. The album sold more than a million copies in a short period of time. This album is the only album in Iran with a sales record that exceeds one million hard copies.

Chavoshi’s second album, “Jacket” was released in February of 2010 by “Iran Gaam Production”. It reached the number one place on the list of most sold albums for the second year in a row. Chavoshi’s album “Greedy”, which is his third official album, was released in February 2011 by “TDH Company”. This album sold three hundred thousand copies within three days. The first edition of the album was scheduled for six hundred thousand copies. Nevertheless, in response to the public demand a second edition was published.

Just a few months after the release of “greedy”, “Santouri’s” soundtrack album was officially launched by the company “Iran Gaam” in Autumn of 2011. Chavoshi’s next album is “The White Flag” which was released in winter of 2012 by two companies “TGP” and “TDH”. In this album Rock genre is evident more than any other of his previous works. This work also displays the artist’s first experimentation with other genres such as Blues and Reggae.

In 2013 “I Myself Am the Thirteen” was released by “TGP Company”. This album had been produced many years prior to its release date. Chavoshi has commented that he was waiting for the right time to launch the album. The work commemorates renowned Iranian poets; “Rumi”, “Shahriar” and “Vahshi Bafqi” by using their classic poems.

Chavoshi’s seventh album was released in October 2014 by “TGP Company”. “Boatless Oar” reached second edition only two days after its release thus breaking an unprecedented record. “Boatless Oar” is a combination of Pop, Rock and Electronic genres mixed by German sound engineer “Chris Bauer”.

Chavoshi’s most recent album “Harmless Ruler” was produced by “TGP Company” and released in June of 2016. The album incorporates works of classic Persian poets and contemporary songwriters. This innovative work has gained great popularity amongst various people with different backgrounds and interests.

Music Career

Chavoshi began his involvement with TV series and movie soundtrack by performing in “Santouri”, a movie directed by “Dariush Mehrjui”. “Santouri” is considered as one of the most popular Iranian movies of the last decade and it has been suggested that the movie’s soundtrack is one of the main reasons for its popularity. “The Patience Stone”, one of the four pieces on the sound track is believed to be one of the Iran’s ten most remarkable songs of all times.

Chavoshi’s second cinematic work is the soundtrack of movie “Lamp 100” directed by “Saeed Aghakhani”. Movie’s theme is about addiction, and since Chavoshi’s priority has always been social concerns such as addiction, peace and poverty, he sang the piece “Willow Without Weeping” for this movie.

In addition to this two movies, he has produced eight musical pieces for the series “Shahrzad” which is one of the most viewed TV series of Iran. Four out of eight pieces were played during episodes and other four have been published as video clips.

Soundtracks

Mohsen Chavoshi has been recognized by Iranian public as well as the media, experts and critics since he launched his music carrier. He was chosen as the most popular Iranian singer in various opinion polls including the one conducted by the Iranian Network that has millions of viewers. He was selected twice as the year outstanding cultural figure by the On-Line News.

In the company of the influential figures such as Javad Zarif, Iran’s Secretary of State and Shahab Hosseini, winner of the best actor award in Cannes Film Festival 2016, Chavoshi achieved the first place on the list of the most influential figures in the popular publication Movafaghiat in 2016.

Amongst Chavoshi’s many awards are the Hafez Prize and the Rumi Plaque that he received for his efforts in honoring the great poet during the 4th Rumi’s commemoration celebration.

Chavoshi‘s achievements include his successes in creating albums and singles that have broken the sale records in the past decade. The public response to Chavoshi’s music has led some music experts to study the influence of his music on the Iranian culture.
